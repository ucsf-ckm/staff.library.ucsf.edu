NEW PATRON SERVICE STANDARDS AND GUIDELINES New standards…

June 4, 2014 by Eric Peterson

NEW PATRON SERVICE STANDARDS AND GUIDELINES

New standards and guidelines for patron service interactions were generated by Public Services staff in meetings near the end of last year. These have been posted next to service points as reminders of what we need to provide our patrons. In addition, Public Services supervisors are observing and coaching staff interaction with patrons in meeting these service standards.

UCSF LIBRARY ACCESS SERVICES
PATRON SERVICE STANDARDS AND GUIDELINES

ACKNOWLEDGE the patron immediately…say “I’ll be right with you” if
you’re busy with another patron.

ENGAGE the patron as soon as you’re available to do so.

DETERMINE the patron’s needs by listening carefully and asking
questions.

RECOGNIZE that the patron may be unfamiliar with library processes.

ANSWER the patron’s question…by being knowledgeable about
library procedures.

TEACH the patron how to accomplish a task rather than just
performing the task for him or her.

DIRECT the patron to alternatives if you’re unable to resolve their
issue.

MAINTAIN your composure…don’t take criticism personally.

SUPPORT your co‐workers when needed to provide excellent
customer service.

INQUIRE if you may assist the patron in any other way.
